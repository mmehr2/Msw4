Registering MagicScroll

After installing and running your copy of MagicScroll, you will be presented with the "Unregistered Copy" dialog box. This form contains information you will need to register MagicScroll and use it without limitations.

Buying MagicScroll

To purchase MagicScroll, you will need an internet connection. From the "Register MagicScroll" command on the Help menu, or in the "Unregistered Copy" dialog box, click on the "Buy Now" button. This will open your internet browser to the Magic Teleprompting web site, where you can purchase the product. You will need a registration name as well as the Computer ID displayed on the dialog box.

Finding Registration Information

If you are asked for your registration name or number, they can be found on the "Register MagicScroll" dialog box. Click on the "Register MagicScroll" command on the Help menu to open this dialog box. Your computer ID can be found here, as well as on the "About MagicScroll" dialog box.
