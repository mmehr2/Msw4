To create a bulleted list

  1. Click where you want the bulleted list to start.
  2. On the Format menu, click Bullet Style, and then type your text. When you press Enter, another bullet appears on the next line.
  3. To end the bulleted list, on the Format menu, click Bullet Style again.

Notes

  • You can also create a bulleted list by clicking the Bullets button on the format bar.
  • To create an indented paragraph in a bulleted list, press Shift+Enter.
