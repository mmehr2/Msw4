To indent a paragraph

  1. Click anywhere in the paragraph you want to format.
  2. To indent the first line, click and drag the first-line indent marker () on the ruler to the position desired.
  3. To indent all except the first line of the paragraph, click and drag the indent marker () on the ruler to the position desired.
  4. To indent the entire paragraph, moving both the first line and indent markers together, click and drag the () button on the ruler.
  5. To set the right border of the paragraph, click and drag the right indent marker () on the ruler to the position desired.

Note

  • You can indent all of the paragraphs in a new document by changing the format options before you begin to type.
