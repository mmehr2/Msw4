View menu commands

The View menu offers the following commands:

Toolbar Shows or hides the toolbar.
Control Panel Shows or hides the control panel.
Format Bar Shows or hides the format bar.
Ruler Shows or hides the ruler.
Status Bar Shows or hides the status bar.
Script Queue Shows or hides the script queue.
Go Into Scroll Mode Causes the scroll window to be displayed.
NTSC Display Update  
Invisibles Shows or hides invisible characters such as space, tab, and new lines.
Line Numbers Shows or hides line number on the cue bar.
