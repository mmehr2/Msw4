Ruler

The ruler allows you to set scroll margins, as well as printing margins, paragraph alignment, and tab stops.

To set the scroll margins, click and drag the horizontal arrows on the top of the ruler. These margins will affect the entire document in the edit window as well as the scroll window.

To set paragraph and printing margins, select the paragraphs to modify, then drag the indent markers to the positions desired. Click the ruler where you want a tab stop to appear. You can delete tab stops by dragging them off the ruler.

Show or hide the ruler using the View | Ruler command.
