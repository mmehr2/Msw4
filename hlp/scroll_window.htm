Scroll Window

When you start your prompting session, scripts will appear in the scroll window, pictured below.

Timer In the top left is the actual run time (ART) timer.
Cue Pointer The cue pointer is to the left of the script. It can be repositioned in the edit window.
Speed Indicator The gray gradient box on the left side of the window indicates current scroll speed. Adjust your speed using a hand controller, the UP and DOWN arrow keys on the keyboard, or your mouse.
Script Position Indicator The rectangle next to the speed indicator shows the current position in the script(s).

Several hot keys are available for navigating your scripts in the scroll window.

Elements and attributes of the scroll window can be adjusted on the Video tab of the Preferences dialog box.
