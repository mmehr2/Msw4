Scroll Window Hot Keys

The following hot keys are available in scroll mode:

Up Bumps the scroll speed up
Down Bumps the scroll speed down
Enter Pauses / Resumes scrolling
   
Home Jumps to the beginning of the current script
End Jumps to the end of the current script
   
Space Sets a paperclip at the cue pointer
Ctrl+Space Jumps to next paperclip
Ctrl+Shift+Space Jumps to previous paperclip
Ctrl+K Jumps to next bookmark
Ctrl+Shift+K Jumps to previous bookmark
   
Ctrl+Shift+T Toggles the ART timer on/off
Ctrl+Alt+T Resets the ART timer to 0
